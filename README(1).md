# Business Sales Analysis Dashboard 📊

An interactive **Business Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Python, HTML, Plotly, and Data Analytics techniques** to analyze revenue trends, top-selling products, customer behavior, and regional sales performance.

This project provides a **client-ready sales analytics report** and an **interactive dashboard** for business decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to analyze online store sales data and generate meaningful business insights such as:

* Revenue trends over time
* Top-performing products
* Regional sales analysis
* Order status impact on revenue
* Marketing channel performance
* Business recommendations for growth

The project includes:

* 📈 Interactive Dashboard
* 📑 Business Analysis Report
* 📊 KPI Tracking
* 🧠 Data-Driven Recommendations

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* High cancellation and return rates are reducing actual revenue.
* Printers, Chairs, and Laptops are the top revenue-generating products.
* West region performs best in overall sales.
* Instagram and Google are strong customer acquisition channels.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Reduce cancellations and returns through better order management.
2. Focus marketing on high-performing products.
3. Improve sales strategy in low-performing regions.
4. Increase investment in high-performing referral channels.
5. Track net revenue instead of only gross revenue.
